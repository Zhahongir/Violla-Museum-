Battle of Agosta

The naval battle that took place on April 22, 1676 near the Sicilian city of Agosta (Augusta, southeastern coast of Sicily) during the Dutch war between a French squadron commanded by Vice Admiral Abraham Duquesne and a Dutch-Spanish squadron commanded by Admirals Michael de Ruyter and Don Francisco de la Cerda.

Battle for Shevardinsky Redoubt

The battle for the Shevardinsky redoubt took place on September 5 (August 24), two days before the Borodino battle. ... Large units of the French army, outnumbered the Russians, were thrown into battle; they had to quickly recapture the position.

Peter the Great Peter I

Peter the Great Peter I — the last Tsar of All Russia and the first Emperor of All Russia. Representative of the Romanov dynasty. He was proclaimed tsar at the age of 10, and began to rule independently from 1689. The formal co-ruler of Peter was his brother Ivan.
